We are presenting a competition in collaboration with the Department of Physical Education (KTV MFF UK). Our goal is to replace traditional Dean's sports day but also to remind everyone that exercising is crucial for maintaining good health, especially in this time of lock downs.

Our sports instructors decided to set several competitions and categories. However, this competition is also about having fun! Together, we all will try to get to the easternmost point of the Czech Republic then Slovakia, and around Europe.

The top competitors and contributors will be rewarded. The best 10 participants will get a t-shirt and the Dean will reward the best 3 participants from each category with a special prize.

Do not lose your hope even if you can't reach the highest places in the leaderboard! You can still earn credit for Optional Physical Education. For more info about the conditions for the credit, check out the FAQ.

We wish good luck to all contestants!

Running - 5 km

Are you fast runner in shorter distances? Submit any run with distance of at least 5 km and you are in. The winner is the one with the shortest average time per kilometer.

This competition is divided into four categories: men under 35, men over 35, women under 35, and women over 35.

Running - 10 km

Can you run fast even in longer distances? Just submit any run with distance of at least 10 km and beat all students and teachers. Again, the shortest average time per kilometer will decide.

This competition is divided into four categories: men under 35, men over 35, women under 35, and women over 35.

Running / Walking

Do you prefer long runs or long walks in dark forests? No problem! You can be the winner in the total distance on foot. Every running and walking activity is counted.

This competition is not divided into categories.

Cycling

Are you the best biker of the world? It doesn't matter whether you ride single tracks somewhere in the mountains or you just ride to the groceries. Track your ride, submit, and win the longest total distance on bike.

This competition is not divided into categories.
