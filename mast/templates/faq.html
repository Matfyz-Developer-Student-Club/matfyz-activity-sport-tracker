Who can participate?

Any student, employee or alumni of MFF UK.

How do I participate?

Well, all you need to do is to complete your profile on the User Settings page, authenticate via Strava in the Integrations and start running. Activities uploaded to STRAVA are automatically sent to Mathletics.

Note that your STRAVA activity is counted only if it is made public in STRAVA.

See rules to see more about the rules.

When does the event takes place?

For this semester, Mathletics takes place from 22. 3. till 30. 6. 2021.

How can I win?

For each recorded activity you will score some points. Then you need to place in top 10 in any category to win a prize. See What can I win? section for more information about prizes.

The score is computed by a sophisticated formula that takes in account your activity distance, pace and elevation gain and also your sex.

What can I win?

First three places in every category win valuable gift basket form MFF.

Also the first ten competitors win a T-shirt.

What are the competition categories?

This season there is five disciplines all together.

  • Walking - any walk longer 3 km
  • Running - any run longer 5 km
  • Inline skating - any trip longer 8 km
  • Cycling - any ride longer 10 km
  • Total - the person with the biggest score sum across all the categories

Rules

  1. You must be registered.
  2. The activity is accounted for only if it satisfies the minimum distance criterion (see above).
  3. The activity can not be interleaved with other activities and must be recorded outside.
  4. Activities obviously related to work duties such as being messenger, courier, food delivery boy will not be accounted.
  5. The organizer (KTV) reserves the right not to recognize activities that drift off reality caused by for instance GPS issues (such cases will be resolved individually).

How do I enter the competitions?

You enter the competitions automatically. All you need to do is authenticate via strava in Integrations and use STRAVA to record your activity.

Any activity on strava that is public will be automatically uploaded to Mathletics.

I have encountered some technical difficulties. How should I report them?

We welcome all bug reports on mdsc@matfyzak.cz.

Additionally, you can contribute to this project and others by joining our club: Matfyz Developer Student Club.
