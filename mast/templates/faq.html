How to I make activity public in STRAVA?

  • In STRAVA go to "Training" and there click "My Activities". This will list all activities on your STRAVA account.
  • Click on "Edit" at activity you want to make public. This will show you the edit UI for the activity.
  • Here under Privacy Controls you can select either "Everyone" or "Followers". Both are fine if you want the activity to be registered at Mathletics.

Common problems:

I uploaded activity to STRAVA but it's not showing anywhere.

  • Make sure your activity is of correct type and satisfies minimum length.
  • The activity must be public. See tutorial to see how to make sure the activity is public.
  • If everything is as it should be. You can try to set the Visibility to Private and Save it. Then right away you can set it back to public. Wait a few minutes and the activity should now be on your profile.
  • If it still is not showing here please email us mdsc@matfyzak.cz and please include link to your STRAVA activity as it helps us solve the problem much faster.

Who can participate?

Any student, employee or alumni of MFF UK.

How long does it take for the activity to load from STRAVA to Mathletics?

It usually takes between 1 to 2 minutes.

Can I somehow upload activities manually and not rely on STRAVA to upload it to Mathletics?

Unfortunatelly no. Due to limited number of requests between STRAVA and Mathletics we have to rely on STRAVA to inform us about your new activities.

If you encounter upload problems, please check Common problems section or email us at mdsc@matfyzak.cz straight away. We will look into it.

How do I participate?

Well, all you need to do is to complete your profile on the User Settings page, authenticate via Strava in the Integrations and start running. Activities uploaded to STRAVA are automatically sent to Mathletics starting March 29.

Note that your STRAVA activity is counted only if it is made public in STRAVA.

How can I win?

For each recorded activity you will score some points. Then you need to place in top 10 in any category to win a prize. See What can I win? section for more information about prizes.

The score is computed by a sophisticated formula that takes in account your activity distance, pace and elevation gain and also your sex.

The Formula:

  • d ... distance
  • a ... scaled elevation [0;1]
  • p ... sex (1 for male; 1,1 for female)
  • t ... scaled pace [0;1]

Note that not every category uses all parameters used in the formula. For more information see About Competitions.

What can I win?

First three participants in each category win equipment worth 700 Kč, 500 Kč or 300 Kč respectively.

Also, the first ten competitors win a MFF T-shirt.

The department of physical education (KTV) picks the best participants in each category based on uploaded activities.

Rules

  1. You must be registered on this website.
  2. You have to authenticate via Strava. Activities uploaded before authentication will not be counted.
  3. Activity must be accessible to public in STRAVA.
  4. The activity is accounted for only if it satisfies the minimum distance criterion (see above).
  5. The activity can not be interleaved with other activities and must be recorded outside.
  6. Activities obviously related to work duties such as being messenger, courier, food delivery boy will not be accounted.
  7. The organizer (KTV) reserves the right not to recognize activities that drift off reality caused by for instance GPS issues (such cases will be resolved individually).

How do I enter the competitions?

You enter the competitions automatically. All you need to do is authenticate via strava in Integrations and use STRAVA to record your activity.

Any activity on STRAVA that is public will be automatically uploaded to Mathletics in about 5 minutes after upload.
